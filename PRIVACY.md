# Privacy Policy for CodeSync Pro

**Last Updated: June 17, 2026**

CodeSync Pro ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled by the extension.

## 1. Information Collected and Stored
CodeSync Pro does **not** collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data, credentials, or browsing history to our own servers or to any third parties. 

All configuration data, including:
- Your GitHub Personal Access Token (PAT)
- Your Codeforces, AtCoder, and LeetCode handles/usernames
- Your repository sync settings and logs

are stored **locally** on your device using the browser's secure `chrome.storage.local` API.

## 2. Third-Party Connections
The extension makes direct HTTPS connections from your browser to the following platforms to perform its core function:
- **GitHub API** (to create files and push your code)
- **Codeforces API** (to fetch your submissions)
- **AtCoder API** (to fetch your submissions)
- **LeetCode API** (to fetch your submissions)

Your credentials (GitHub token) are sent directly to GitHub and are never shared with us or any other server.

## 3. Data Deletion
Since all data is stored locally in your browser, you can permanently delete all stored data at any time by:
- Clearing the extension's storage in your browser settings.
- Uninstalling the extension.
